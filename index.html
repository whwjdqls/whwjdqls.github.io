Research

My long-term goal is to build a human foundation model: an embodied system that perceives, moves, and interacts with its surroundings and with people, whether as a digital human or a humanoid robot.

I believe perception is inherently shaped for behavior, so we should build models that capture the perception–action loop (i.e., active perception). Such models should not only learn to see and move, but also reason about world dynamics and, in turn, interact with their surrounding environment. I’m very excited to see what emergent, human-like behaviors will arise from these models. Would they begin to behave—just like us? I aim to explore these directions during my PhD.
